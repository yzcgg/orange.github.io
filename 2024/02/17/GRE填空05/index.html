GRE填空05

test 02

1

The problem a generation ago was not the new art itself but the heightened intensity of conviction surrounding it, which is thankfully absent in our wonderfully __________ times.

A. fervid
B. impassioned
C. undogmatic
D. receptive
E. nostalgic
F. optimistic

  • conviction 除了 定罪 还有 坚信,深信 的意思
  • fervid 强烈的, 热烈的
  • impassioned 充满激情的,热烈的

一代人之前的问题不是由艺术品本身产生的, 而是由围绕在它周围的坚定的信仰产生的, 好在现在这个美好的 不教条的,接受的 时代, 这种坚信缺席了.

2

Given that the department director was such a feeble contributor, sitting silently at important policy meetings and usually deferring to low-level aides, it was surprising that he had such a reputation for __________.

A. magnanimity
B. perspicacity
C. impartiality
D. detachment
E. benevolence
F. discernment

  • feeble 虚弱的, 衰弱的
  • defer to sb. 遵从,听从,顺从
  • perspicacious 有洞察力的
  • discern 察觉出,分辨出
  • detachment 公正,客观
  • impartial 公正的, 不偏不倚的

3

Although torrential rains can sometimes be highly destructive, human survival generally proves much more __________ without rain than during periods of its overabundance.

A. ponderous
B. unmanageable
C. onerous
D. tedious
E. arduous
F. unlikely

  • torrent n.激流,急流,狂潮
  • ponderous adj.笨重的,缓慢的
  • onerous adj.费劲的
  • arduous adj.艰难的

意思是: 尽管大雨有时很具有破坏性, 但是幸存下来的人们都说, 有些(由大雨引起的)困难比发大水的时候更加费劲.

4

Consolidating a memory is not instantaneous or even __________: every memory must be encoded and moved from short-term to long-term storage, and some of those memories are, for whatever reason, more vividly imprinted than others.

A.salutary
B.deliberate
C.sequential
D.momentary
E.Inevitable

  • salutary 有益的 同根词:
  • consolidate v.使巩固,使加强
  • imprint v.刻上记号,铭记
    【过往命中时间】2023年03月11日中国线上；2022年08月15日中国线上；2021.08.20中国陆线下；2020-09-11，2017-08-25, 2018-09-07, 2018-07-05, 2018-06-07，2021年09月01日中国线上
    【答案】E
    【解析】思路一：or在并列否定句时，not是在or前后共用的，所以第一句话的完整内容应该是：consolidating memory is not instantaneous or even 【not】 ___ , 冒号后面是解释内容，every memory must be encoded and moved from short-term to long-term storage体现的是consolidating需要一个过程，对应not instantaneous, 第二句话some of these memories are, for whatever reason, more vividly imprinted than others体现some 能记住，other不一定能记住，所以是not inevitable(并不是每一段记忆 都会被巩固)。
    思路二：否定递进是弱递进，所以这里的逻辑是“不是立即发生，甚至都不是一定发生的”，所以正确答案选 E 选项。inevitable 必然的。
    【句子翻译】巩固记忆不是瞬间发生的甚至都不是一定会发生的：每一个记忆必须被编码而且从短期变成长期的储存，而且一些记忆，出于某些原因，比其他记忆铭刻得更加清晰。

5

The argument, while important, combines analysis and __________, swinging in tone from patient consideration to bitter invective.

A. introspection
B. prevarication
C. facetiousness
D. idealism
E. Polemic

  • swing v.摇摆 n.秋千
  • bitter adj.激烈的,苦的
  • invective n.咒骂,谩骂
  • introspect v.内省,反省
  • prevaricate v.支支吾吾,搪塞,闪烁其词
  • facetious adj.(在不当的场合)开玩笑的; 滑稽的; 轻浮的
  • polemic n.激烈争论,论战,辩论术

逗号前后取同，空格对应bitter invective取同，选择E。

6

Gutierrez is the only one who seems to be (i)__________ the controversy sparked by his article: his appetite for scientific (ⅱ)__________ seems, if anything, to be increasing with age.

relishing
quelling
whitewashing

altercations
conundrums
breakthroughs

  • quell v.镇压,平息;消除,缓解
  • whitewash v.粉饰,掩饰;在体育比赛中接连击败对手的）全胜，完胜
  • altercation n.吵嘴,争吵
  • conundrum n.令人迷惑的难题

古铁雷斯似乎是唯一一个喜欢他的文章引发的争议的人:如果有什么不同的话，他对科学难题的兴趣似乎随着年龄的增长而增加。

altercation是口角，激烈的争吵的意思，G享受争议，是因为他喜欢科学的难题，并不是说他喜欢在科学问题上与别的观点争吵。

7

Although it is quite probable that many small asteroids in the solar system’s asteroid belt have (i), the total mass of these objects cannot be (ⅱ), since the combined gravitational pull of all known asteroids is (ⅲ)____ to fully account for the asteroid belt’s effect on the motion of nearby planets such as Mars.

similar structures
formed by accretion
escaped detection

diminishing
estimated
substantial

sufficient
unlikely
required

  • mass n.质量(重量)
  • asteroid n.小行星
  • diminish v.减少,减弱 同根词: dim
  • accretion n.堆积,堆积层
    【解析】此题偏难，三空都有一定的联动关系，these objects指代前文提到的many small asteroids，整体逻辑是：虽然很多小行星没有被探测到，但是【这些被漏掉的小行星】总质量不会很大，因为已知部分的总引力已经足以解释整个行星带对附近的影响。【即已知的量和真实的量相差不大→没有被探测到的量很小】
    【句子翻译】虽然很有可能太阳系行星带里还有很多小行星没有被探测到，但是这些小行星的总质量不会很大。因为(目前)所有已知的小行星的总引力足以解释小行星带对附近行星(比如火星)运动产生的影响。

8

Our contemporary preference for natural expression over artifice is (i)________ the traditional Western view that the skill with which you expressed a position corresponded closely to the credibility of your argument. Rhetorical styles might vary, but style itself was never a matter of (ⅱ). And“style”was not just a well-turned sentence: poor expression, it was believed, (ⅲ) poor thought.
A.a departure from
B.a reflection on
C.an endorsement of

D.substance
E.indifference
F.intention

G.ameliorated
H.elevated
I.betrayed

  • endorse v.公开支持,赞同
    第一句话主要说的是当代人喜欢无技巧的自然表达（Our contemporary preference for natural expression over artifice）；而传统西方的观点是表达技巧很重要（traditional Western view that the skill of your argument）。这两种观点是对立的，第一空答案选择A. a departure form 远离，脱离。
    but表示转折，but前说修辞手法会改变（vary），but后转折取反选择E indifference。表达风格本身永远不会是无关紧要的，这里的a matter of indifference 是无关紧要的意思。
    最后我们看三空，冒号解释说明。冒号前说style不仅仅是一个华丽的句子，那么冒号后表示匮乏的表达也反应了匮乏的思想，所以第三空答案选择I betray “表明”的意思
    句子翻译: 我们当代对自然表达的偏好超过了技巧，这背离了传统的西方观点，即你表达立场的技巧与你的论点的可信度密切相关。修辞风格可能会有所不同，但风格本身从来都不是无关紧要的。而且“风格”不仅仅是一句得体的句子:人们相信，拙劣的表达暴露了拙劣的思想。
