GRE模考01

昨天模考了一次verbal, 149, 还差不少, 这里复盘一些我觉得还算正常的题目.

填空

1

Trilobites,with their famously distinctive lobed body plans and curious appurtenances, are____fossils,the perfect symbols of an archaic world.

A. archetypal
B. elusive
C. obscure
D. prevalent
E. transitory

选A, 我选对了, 就是因为后面的symbol 让我觉得应该对应 archetypal.

  • appurtenance n.附属物

2

The scientist’s disagreement with the mainstream scientific predictions about climate change is (i) _________. Unlike global-warming skeptics, however, he believes that the consensus view actually (ii)_________the problem.

sharp
technical
resolvable

downplays
captures
overstates

第一空选sharp（与主流观点很不同）；however取反，与其他人不同，他认为主流观点抓住了问题的所在。

【这位科学家与主流科学对气候变化的预测大相径庭。然而，与全球变暖怀疑论者不同，他认为共识观点实际上抓住了问题所在。】

这题就纯考逻辑, 这逻辑也够逆天的, 把unlike的那半句话去掉, 说得通, 前面说这位科学家强烈反对主流观点, however转折, 后面说科学家认为在某一个问题上, 主流观点抓住了问题所在.

3

Nowadays, the conventions held by United States political parties to nominate presidential candidates are little more than carefully orchestrated coronation ceremonies leading to preordained outcomes, but there was a time when nominating conventions were truly_______.

A. suspenseful
B. contentious
C. stately
D. dignified
E. combative
F. momentous

  • nominate v.提名
  • orchestrate v.精心安排
  • coronation n.加冕礼
  • preordained adj.注定的
  • little more than 仅仅是 这个表示逻辑关系的词组是我做错的关键, little其实表示否定含义了, 只多一点儿, 其实就是: 仅仅是
  • dignify v.使有尊严,使崇高v
  • stately adj.庄严的,宏大的

4

Elizabethan actor Richard Tarlton was so____in appearance that a mere glimpse of his grinning face peeping through a curtain was enough to trigger prolonged peals of laughter.

A. animated adj.生机勃勃的,活跃的
B. risible adj.可笑的
C. comely adj.标致的;秀丽的
D. Ludicrous adj.荒唐的,滑稽的,可笑的
E. handsome
F. anomalous

逻辑较简单, 推出空格处应填入 可笑的 含义. 选择BD.

5

A. copious
B. overwhelming
C. meager
D. paltry
E. abundant

6

The philosopher was a strong advocate of _____: he taught that happiness comes from forgoing one’s desires.

B. duty

1

Findings in biological anthropology suggest that Native Americans’ ancestors probably came to the Americas from northeastern Asia. Turner, for example, has studied the dentition of prehistoric and living Native Americans and northeastern Asians. Based on approximately twenty dental traits, including tooth-crown shape and number of roots, he has defined an overall dental pattern called Sinodonty, which includes three-rooted lower first molars and shovel-shaped incisors. This distinctive pattern is shared among most Native Americans and people from northeastern Asia, but is not found in people who originated in southern Asia, Africa, or Europe. Another less complex pattern — Sundadonty —is shared among the people of southeastern Asia and is also found in prehistoric American populations, but Turner believes that widespread Sinodonty demonstrates most Native Americans’ northeastern Asian origins.

第一句: 美国祖先可能来自亚洲东北. 人类生物学家观点.
第二句: 一个人T, 研究了 史前 和 现在 美国本土人 以及 亚洲东北部人的 牙齿.
第三句: 基于20个牙齿的特点, 包括…, 他定义出一种叫: Sinodonty 的模式, 该模式….
第四句: 这个特殊的模式, 美国本土人 和 亚洲东北人都有, 但是起源于亚洲南部, 非洲或欧洲的人没有.
第五句: 另一个没那么明显的模式 Sundadonty 亚洲东南人和史前的美国人都有, 但是T认为 Sinodonty证明 美国本土人和亚洲东北人 是 一个 起源.

2

Canali, lava channels on Venus thousands of kilometers long (compared to Earth’s sinuous rilles of a few hundred kilometers) and 1 to 2 kilometers wide, have perplexed scientists. One interpretation of canali origins is that ordinary basaltic silicate lava formed them. Silicate lava is quite erosive initially but rapidly cools and solidifies. Calculations indicate that Venus’ dense atmosphere would accelerate convective cooling, preventing these lavas from flowing thousands of kilometers if erupted in channel-eroding quantities. Alternatively, the volume of lava needed to flow the required distance would have buried the channels we now observe. Therefore, silicate lavas probably cannot form canali. In another interpretation, canali-forming lavas had low viscosity, therefore high mobility, and temperatures closer to their Venusian surroundings, thereby decreasing convective cooling. No silicate lavas possess this combination, but rare carbonatite lavas exhibit both properties: melting points around 500 degrees Celsius and viscosities like water’s. Such lava on Venus would harden slowly,allowing sediments to sink and channels to erode laterally.

第一句: Canli 是 金星上 岩浆通道 上千公里长 1 - 2公里宽, 相比于地球的 几百千米的弯曲的 通道. 让科学家们困惑.

  • sinuous 弯曲的,蜿蜒的
  • Venus 金星

第二句: 关于 Canali 起源的解释之一 是 一种 obs 的 lava 形成了它们.
第三句: 这种lava 刚开始很具有 侵蚀性 但 很快就冷却并且凝固.
第四句: 计算表明 金星 浓密的大气层 会 加速冷却, 阻止这些 lava流淌上千千米,
第五句: 或者说, 如果喷发的量特别大足以流淌上千公里, 大量的lava可能会把我们当前观测到的通道掩埋起来.

  • alternatively adj.引出第二种选择或可能的建议:要不,或者

第六句: 所以, 这种s lava或许不能形成canali.
第七句: 另一种解释是: 形成canali的岩浆 黏性很低, 流动性很强, 并且温度接近于金星表面, 因此可以减缓冷却.

  • viscosity n.黏性
  • mobility n.流动能力, 活动性

第八句: s lava不 同时具有这些属性, 但是 rc lava展示出全部这些属性: 熔点在500度左右 + 黏性就像水.
最后一句: 这种lava可以在金星上缓慢的变硬, 这就使得 沉淀物沉积, 并且进一步侵蚀形成隧道.

  • sediment 沉淀物, 沉积物

3

In the United States, studying the territoriality of nonnative ants such as the red fire ant, a species native to South America, may-seem unlikely to yield generalizable results-but it does have distinct advantages. Most importantly, whereas the fire ant shares habitat with many ant competitors in South America, its communities in the United States are much simpler. Colonizable landscape is blanketed almost continuously with fire ant territories. so all territorial boundaries are with other fire ant colonies. We can study the complexities of territoriality in almost pure, continuous populations of a single species, without the complicating influences of other competitors. Furthermore, the high densities of many fire ant populations ensure that interactions among competing colonies will be intense and obvious.

  1. 对一种 非美国本土的 原产自南美的 红火蚂蚁 的研究, 看起来很难产生通用性的结果, 但是确实又独特的优势.
  2. whereas 然而, 但是. 最重要的是, 尽管红火蚂蚁 在南美 与很多竞争者共享栖息地., 它们在南美的社区简单得多.
  3. 全部被 红火蚁 的领地覆盖, 所以所有的边界 都是和其他 火蚁 的领地.
  4. 我们可以研究一个物种的连续的领地, 而不受其他竞争者的影响.
  5. 不仅如此, 火蚁的 高密度 确保了 竞争领地之间的交流将会是 紧密而明显的.
  • colony n.殖民地
  • territory n.领土
  • blanket n.毛毯 v.覆盖
