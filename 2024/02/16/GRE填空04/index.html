test01

1

One thing both authors have in common is a striking amount of _____: they claim to know how massive institutions, some of them richly endowed, all of them central to American society and culture, should be reshaped.
A hubris
B propriety
C bias
D prescience
E indolence

【过往命中时间】2022年08月11日中国线上；
【答案】A
【解析】冒号后面的内容说他们说话有种夸大的感觉，能对应这种特征的只能是 A 选项。hubris 傲慢自大。
【句子翻译】极其自命清高是每个作家都具有的共同特点，他们声称知道那些处于美国文化社会中心的，拥有得天独厚资源的庞大机构应该如何重塑。
【选项释义】
hubris 傲慢
propriety 得体的举止,礼仪 n.
bias 偏见
prescience 预知
indolence 懒惰 n. indolent 懒惰的

  • endow v.(向学校等机构)捐钱;
  • be endowed with sth. 天生赋予,生来就有(某种品质)

2

Although the essayist`s arguments did not _____ her most perceptive readers, the extreme subtlety of the points she made explains why she was misinterpreted by most critics of her day.
A convince
B confound
C entertain
D persuade
E perplex
F enlighten

【过往命中时间】2024年02月01日中国线上；2023年05月06日北美线上；2019-08-31
【答案】B E
【解析】让步关系
虽然这个作者的论点没有_______ 她大部分的有洞察力的读者，极度难懂的笔触解释了为什么她被同时代大部分的批评家误解。
后半句——误解，前半句——没误解，选BE,使困惑
【句子翻译】尽管这个作者的议论并没有让她最敏锐的读者感到困惑，但她描绘出的极端难懂的内容解释了她为什么被她同时期大部分文学评论家所误解。
【选项释义】
A. convince 使信服
B. confound 使迷惑
C. entertain 娱乐
D. persuade 劝说
E. perplex 使困惑
F. enlighten v.启发,开导

3

One of the vocalists who auditioned for a leading part in the local production of Sweeney Todd seemed to prefer _____ to any attempt at producing a melody; a more unpleasant voice was hard to imagine.

A. warbling
B. imitating
C. improvising
D. shrieking
E. crooning
F. Caterwauling

【过往命中时间】2020-09-11，2017-05-21,
【答案】D F
【解析】a more unpleasant voice was hard to imagine 的意思是“很难想想一个更难听的声音了”，所以空格要选体现不好听的特征，正确答案选 DF 选项。shrieking 尖叫，caterwauling 尖叫。
【句子翻译】一位来竞争本地剧作《理发师陶德》主演的歌手，一张嘴就开始嚎叫，很难比这唱得更难听了。
【选项释义】
A. warble v.鸟啭,用颤音唱
B. imitating 模仿
C. improvising
D. shriek 尖叫
E. croon 低声歌唱
F. caterwaul (像猫一样)嚎叫

  • audition n.v.试唱,试音,试演
  • vocalist 歌手,声乐家
  • vocal 嗓音的,声音的;直言不讳的
  • improvise 即兴创作,临时做

4

Though humanitarian emergencies are frequent features of television news, such exposure seldom________ the public, which rather seems resigned to a sense of impotency.

A.paralyzes
B.demoralizes
C.assuages
D.galvanizes
E.exasperates

【过往命中时间】 2021年11月07日中国线下；2021年10月10日中国线下；2019-10-20, 2017-04-14,
【答案】D
【解析】which rather seems resigned to a sense of impotency 说明 seldom+ 空格=resigned，所以空格选 resigned 的反义，正确答案选 D 选项。galvanize激励。
【句子翻译】尽管新闻报道中的人道主义危机是时常发生的，这样的曝光很少刺激公众，反而有种看似无力的感觉。
【选项释义】
A. paralyzes v.使瘫痪,使麻痹
B. demoralizes 使泄气,使士气低落 morale 士气
C. assuages 平息,缓和
D. galvanizes 刺激
E. exasperates 激怒

  • feature （报章、电视等的）特写，专题节目
  • resign v.辞职
  • impotent adj.无力的,阳痿的

5

Museum re-creations of seventeenth-century New England interiors reflect the assumption that Puritan culture was__________: the room are sparsely furnished, sparely decorated, and dimly lighted.

A.industrious
B.profligate
C.garish
D.credulous
E.Austere

  • sparse 稀少的
  • garish 花哨的
  • credulous 轻信的,易受骗的

6

Weeds lack the lethality of insects and microbes, which can strike farm crops seemingly out of nowhere and rapidly wipe them out. It is worth bearing in mind, however, that infestations of insects and disease are typically (i)_________ events, whereas weeds are(ii)_________.

A.sporadic
B.devastating
C.intractable

D.resilient
E.controllable
F.ubiquitous

选 AF

  • lethal 致命的,致死的 lethality n.致命
  • weed 杂草
  • sporadic 不经常发生的,偶尔发生的
  • resilient 可迅速恢复的,有弹性的
  • devastate 毁灭,彻底破坏
  • intractable 难对付的

7

To avoid being brought down by the financial scandal that rocked the government, Jackson sought to (i)__herself. Along with her staff, she prepared and issued carefully worded statements admitting and detailing her involvement in the financial dealings at issue but claiming that this involvement was entirely (ii).

exculpate
celebrate
distract

pecuniary
fabricated
unwitting

  • sought – seek
  • celebrate v.庆祝
  • fabricate v.捏造,编造
  • unwitting adj.不知情的,无意的,糊里糊涂地
  • witless adj.愚蠢的,傻的

8

Observations from the Chandra X-ray Observatory give astronomers even more evidence that galaxies (i)__________ and that even their central supermassive black holes eventually (ⅱ)__________.

swell
coalesce
endure

merge
implode
deteriorate

答案是: coalesce merge

  • swell v.膨胀,肿胀;增加,增大
  • coalesce v.合并,汇聚
  • endure v.持续;忍受,忍耐
  • implode v.向内聚爆

9

Cole makes the argument that while some advocates of government transparency seem to treat any exposure of state secrets as an (i)_, that position is(ii)____ ;there are many legitimate bases for(iii)_________ disclosures, Cole contends, particularly when they reveal the identities of sources and methods of foreign intelligence.

abuse of power
ambiguous art
unmitigated good

untenable
understandable
commonplace

condemning
condoning
extolling

科尔认为，虽然一些提倡政府透明的人似乎把任何国家机密的曝光都视为一件好事，但这种立场是站不住脚的;科尔认为，谴责披露有许多合理的依据，尤其是当它们暴露了外国情报来源和方法的身份时。
选: unmitigated good, untenable, condemning

  • advocate v.提倡; n.提倡者
  • transparency 透明
  • unmitigated 完全的,十足的,彻底的 —- mitigate v.缓和,减轻
  • extol v.赞美
  • condone v.宽恕,纵容
  • intelligence n.情报

10

The Holy People in Navajo sacred narratives do not act as moral __________: when they teach, it is as often by what they do wrong as by what they do right.

A. agents
B. arbiters
C. defenders
D. paragons
E. ethicists
F. Exemplars

在纳瓦霍人的神圣叙事中，圣人并没有扮演道德模范的角色:当他们教导时，他们做错的事情和做对的事情一样多
选 DF

  • narrative n.叙述,讲故事
  • agent n.代理人, 经纪人
  • ethicist n.伦理学家 ethic n.伦理,道德
  • arbiter 仲裁者,决定者,裁决者 arbitrary 任意的,武断的,专断的
