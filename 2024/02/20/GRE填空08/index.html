GRE填空08

范围大概是张巍1700中section63-70左右的单空题

1

1.Anthropologist Jane Goodall was___ in her determination to anthropomorphize the animals she observed with such empathy, and so resisted her editors attempt to recast her descriptions in more dispassionate language.

A. fickle 易变的；无常的
B. stalwart 忠诚的；忠实的
C. solicitous 忠诚的,忠实的,坚定的
D. pretentious 做作的,自命不凡的
E.whimsical

  • empathy 同情, 共鸣 n.
  • dispassionate 不带感情的

解析：so resisted体现了JG的坚定，正确答案选B选项。stalwart坚定的。

翻译：人类学家JG的非常坚定地赋予她用同情观察的动物以人性，而且如此抵制她编辑想要用更加不带感情的语言改写她的描述的企图。

2

2.Although New York exhilarated him, even at first Leger’s reaction to it was not__: he was initially bothered by its stunning verticality.
A. unspontaneous
B. unintentional
C. unqualified
D. unhopeful
E. uninterested

  • exhilarate 使高兴, 使振奋

解析：not+空格对应前面的exhilarate取反义，或者可以说not+空格和bothered取同义，所以正确答案选C。unqualified不受限制的。

翻译：尽管纽约让他很兴奋，但在最初Leger对于纽约的反应不是没有限制的：他最初因为纽约惊人的垂直设计感到烦扰。

感受: 这个题还真让我分析对了, 先分析取同还是取反, 然后发现五个选项只有unqualified稍微有点儿沾边, 其他的带进去都和分析的相反, 就选出来了.

3

1.Discussions of impending water shortages are often couched in apocalyptic rhetoric, yet if the language is somewhat_, the basic message is sound: water is indeed scarce and growing scarcer.

A. abstract
B. complacent
C.ambiguous
D. unfamiliar
E.overblown

  • coach (用某种文体或方式)表达，措辞
  • complacent 自满的

解析：同义重复文中的couched in apocalyptic rhetoric，正确答案选E选项。overblown过分的。

翻译：关于即将发生的水资源短缺经常被表达成世界末日一样的夸张，但即使这些话说的有些过分，但基本的信息还是正确的：水资源的确短缺且正在变得更加短缺。

4

1.The physical layout of the laboratory, although well adapted to the research being pursued when it was built, was not_, making a thorough redesign necessary before a proposed new
experimental program could be undertaken.

A.compromised
B. imprecise
C.convoluted
D.ubiquitous
E.plastic

解析：making a thorough redesign necessary说明实验室之前没有改变过，再根据空格前面的not，得出空格选体现改变意思的词，正确答案选E。plastic可塑的，能变化的。

翻译：实验室的物理布局，尽管一开始建造之时能够满足所有研究所需，但不能改造，这使得在一个新的实验项目开始之前制定一个重新设计是完全有必要的。

5

2.Within the field of emotional intelligence research, disagreements remain about whether emotional intelligence can be learned and strengthened or is____ characteristic.

A. an expressed
B. an inborn
C. an invented
D. a cultivated
E. a perceptible adj.可察觉的,可感知的

解析：此题逻辑非常简单，空格和learned and strengthened取反义词，正确答案选B选项。inborn天生的。

翻译：在情商研究的领域中，关于情商究竟是后天加强学习的还是先天具有的特征仍存在争议。

6

1.The cognitive flexibility of successful fictional detectives is often____ by their cultural ambivalence: detectives’ intellectual acumen, it seems, exists in direct proportion to their uneasy place in society.

A.amplified
B. highlighted
C. spurred
D. matched
E. negated negate v.否定

  • ambivalent （忧喜参半、好坏参半等）矛盾情绪的
  • acumen 聪明
  • proportion 比例

7

2.Barry was a skilled___: though his arguments were completely flawed, they were clever-sounding enough to deceive everyone who heard them.

A. sophist 诡辩家
B. scholar
C. quibbler quibble v.(为小事)计较,斤斤计较,吹毛求疵
D. doctrinaire
E. pedant

  • deceive v.欺骗
