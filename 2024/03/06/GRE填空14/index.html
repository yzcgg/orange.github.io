XGsC0111

The fact that no single Cantonese dictionary is seen as____is one of the main reasons why most Hong Kong authors do not depend on formal reference tools when writing in Cantonese.

A) dependable
B) authoritative
C) meticulous
D) indefinite
E) topical
F) comprehensive

AB. dependable adj.可靠的 authoritative adj.权威的

  • Cantonese n.广东话

xGSC0112

Because it has usually been impossible to eradicate exotic species once they have become established, it is____ to minimize introductions of species that have substantial probabilities of unwanted impacts.

A) prudent
B) customary
C) provident adj.有先见之明的
D) superfluous
E) essential
F) pointless

选AC. C明显比E要更好.

  • eradicate v.根除, 消灭

xGSC0113

The biographer laments the___that abound in texts from antiquity they are so prevalent, she says that they nearly amount to a “conspiracy of silence.”
A) fallacies n.谬误
B) myths
C) misrepresentations
D) elisions n.省略
E) lacunae n.缺失
F) distortions n.曲解

  • lament n. 挽歌，悼文 v. 对……感到悲痛，对……表示失望；抱怨
  • conspiracy n. 阴谋，密谋；阴谋集团
  • antiquity n. 古代

选DE. 复习单词了.

xGSC0114

Meteorology is one of the few fields of applied science that demands prediction; since prediction involves considerable uncertainty and uncertainty is ___scientists, meteorology will continue to exist in a
fraught intellectual space.

A) mysterious to
B) anathema to
C) discredited by
D) pivotal for
E) repellent to
F) familiar to

  • anathema n.可恨的事物
  • repellent adj.令人讨厌的
  • pivotal adj.中枢的,关键的

选BE. 讨厌的.

xGsc0115

Faced with so much conflicting information on the internet, many people come to believe that all sources are so____ that efforts to discriminate between the true and the false are inevitably futile.
A) slanted
B) imprecise
C) splenetic
D) tendentious
E) surreptitious
F) acrimonious

  • ambivalent adj.有矛盾情绪的
  • acrimonious adj.尖刻的

选AD. 有偏见的, 有倾向性的.

xGSC0116

This novel is not great literature: there are too many characters, too many subplots, and no clearly defined protagonist, making for___
story.

A) a banal
B) a diffuse adj.冗长的, 含混不清的
C) a mawkish
D) an innocuous
E) an amorphous adj.无定形的
F) a hackneyed

xGSC0117

Over the past hundred years, numerous experiments on elementary particles have___the classical paradigm of a causal, deterministic universe by showing that some natural phenomena are truly random.

A) augmented
B) supported
C) upended
D) investigated
E) undermined
F) probed – probe v.探查,盘问

  • casual adj. 休闲的
  • causal adj. 因果的

选CE. 因为前面说宇宙是因果和确定的, 后面说证明了一些现象真的是随机的. 所以应该填写 推翻, 损害.

xGSC0118

A) complex
B) intricate

D) malleable
E) ephemeral
F) pliable adj.柔韧的,柔软的

选DF. 中遇蒙对一个. F选项 pliable注意含义.

xGSCo122

The author argues that by setting the agenda, constraining the issues discussed, and crafting “talking points,” political elites are ultimately able to____ the the choices available to voters.

xGSC0123

Laboratory experiments testing whether memories can be purposely erased or implanted seem decidedly____ compared to the sensational depictions of memory manipulation in movies, but such research has nevertheless yielded dramatic results.

xGSC0132

The scholars’ hypothesis challenges the scope of human psychological and behavioral universals, but does not dispense with them entirely: the team remains confident that displays of pride, for instance, are___.

A) inherited
B) pernicious adj.有害的
C) ubiquitous
D) symptomatic adj.有症状的,作为征兆的
E) detrimental
F) omnipresent

  • dispense with sth. 摈弃,不再需要

选 CF.

翻译: 学者们的假设挑战了人类心理和行为共性的范围，但并没有完全废除它们:例如，研究小组仍然相信，骄傲的表现无处不在

E) conducive to 有助于

  • censorship n.审查,检查
