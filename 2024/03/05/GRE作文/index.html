方法论

观点对错 和 政策后果 都是分情况讨论.
观点我们就看他在什么情况下对, 什么情况下不对, 对的情况下多我们就支持, 反之我们就反对.
政策后果我们就看他有哪些好的影响, 有哪些坏的影响, 好的影响多我们就支持, 反之我们就反对.

claim 和 reason 其实就是 一个该不该 + 一个是不是 (总之可以看成两个独立的issue). 带让步的反对就是反对一个, 支持另一个.
比如一个题目: 所有决定都应该交给政治家和政府官员. 原因是: 他们具有更多信息. 所以相比于普罗大众, 可以做出更准确更好的判断.
观点: 支持事实:官员就是比老百姓的信息更多. 但是并不是所有的政治家都会使用这些信息用于做出对社会有利的决定. 所以并不是所有决定都应该交给政治家来做.

敌方观点: 就是说, 不仅要使劲说自己的观点好, 还要留一手, 写敌方观点怎么反驳.

比较下面两个论证:

1.困为飞沫传播是新冠肺炎传播的重要途径，所以我们在任何时候都要戴口罩.

2.诚然吃饭、独处或者在人烟稀少的户外可以不戴口罩，但因为飞沫传播是新冠肺炎传播的重要途径，所以我们在大部分时候还是要戴口罩

承认敌方观点的合理性, 坚持己方观点的优越性—让步.

极端词. – 去掉 极端词的观点支持, 但是 加上极端词的观点, 反对. — 让步.

As people rely more and more on technology to solve problems, the ability of humans to think for themselves will surely deteriorate.

Write a response in which you discuss the extent to which you agree or disagree with the statement and explain your reasoning for the position you take. In developing and supporting your position, you should consider ways in which the statement might or might not hold true and explain how these considerations shape your position.

题目说: 人们越来越依赖科技解决问题, 人类独立思考的能力肯定会受到损害.

我现在要来驳斥它.

作文

To defy the remorseless conditions of the natural world, each species must have a means of ensuring survival. These means exist in a remarkable range of forms such as agility, defensive structures,or,in humanity’s case,mental acuity. For mankind, survival has always depended on the ability to solve problems. And one problem solving tool that has benefited mankind since its inception is the invention of technology. The record of human history has demonstrated that humanity has relied increasingly on technology while showing no deterioration of mental facility. Therefore, the statement that the ability of humans to think for themselves will be hindered by increased reliance on technology is fallacious,because it is indisputable that mankind has for the duration of its existence relied more and more on technology to solve problems while at the same time the ability of humans to think for themselves has increased or at the very least remained at the same level.

The answer to this problem was, of course, the development of new technologies. Tools were developed to fell trees and fashion simple yet protective dwellings;clothing was made of animal furs and hides to retain warmth. Fire was harnessed to provide heat as well as increased nourishment through the cooking of food.

题目

To understand the most important characteristics of a society, one must study its major cities.

作文

The world today has witnessed an ever-increasing pace of urbanization.
Such a fast pace has led some people to believe that one has to study the major cities in order to fully understand the society’s most important characteristics.

The globalization has brought people of different countries much closer than ever before. In this process, people encountered new cultures and communities, prompting the question how we could best understand the most important characteristics of a society.
The world today has been witnessing an ever-increasing pace of urbanization, leading some people to believe that one has to study the major cities in order to fully understand the society’s most important characteristics.
From my point of view, it is admittedly true that from those giant cities we can learn a lot about a culture. However, the most important characteristics of a society might not be observed in the cities, but rather must sometimes be sought in the countryside.

让步段
First of all, some people argue that the most important characteristics of a society can only be found in major cities, which I can partially agree.

The reason is manifold. One important aspect is that the capital of a nation is almost always located in major cities, such as Beijing, Tokyo, and Washington D.C.
Another reason is that the major cities are most likely the hubs of economic activities and most people lives.
For example, to understand the rapid economic development of China since the “Reform and opening up” policy, the best way is to look at the futuristic skylines of major cities like Shenzhen and Shanghai.
This example shows the power of major cities in terms of showing the key characteristics of a society.

反对段
That being said, I do not believe that all of the most important characteristics of a society can be found in major cities, for the reason that many culture elements of those societies come from countryside. not the major cities.
The reason is quite self-evident: the majority of global population has been living in the countryside long before urbanization took off in the recent decades.
Take the United States for example. One of the defining features of American culture is her obsession with firearms, which has even been codified by the Second Amendment. Yet, if one studies the American cities, most of which are strongly for strict gun controls, he or she cannot grasp this crucial aspect of the American society.
As a consequence, only studying the major cities may not fully reveal the most important characteristics of a society, which should in turn be sought in the countryside.

To summarize, while I do agree to some extent that a megacity has a lot to offer for anyone interested in understanding the society, cities do not always hold the key to those most important characteristics of a society. Such characteristics must be found elsewhere.
