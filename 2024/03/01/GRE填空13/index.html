section 111

1.Unable to escape their own literary tradition, literary critics either become the____ of that tradition or, on the contrary, use their knowledge of it to reinterpret writers and trends from new perspectives.

A. liberators B. guardians C. successors D.antithesis E.gadflies

选B.
跟我分析的一致: 中间说正相反, 后面说另一派人是 重新解释, 然后形成新的观点, 前面的一派人自然是 旧的, 那b选项, 保卫者 合理.

  • gadfly n.牛虻；讨厌的人

2.Not only was Jarry___ the pandemonium his play sparkled, he actually worked to foment the conflict,organizing a posse to boo if the rest of the audience applauded and cheer if the other attendees booed.

A. riled by
B. surprised by
C. unperturbed by
D. critical of
E. detached from

  • pandemonium n.骚动；群情沸腾
  • foment vt. 煽动；挑起
  • boo v.发嘘声，喝倒彩
  • perturb v.使…焦虑
  • unperturbed adj.镇静的, 没有被干扰的
  • rile v.惹恼；激怒
  • detach v.拆卸；（使）分开，脱离

3.Boreal forest is at the southern boundary of the moss-dominated tundra,which remains
characteristically treeless because its spongy surface retains water that cannot drain away through the underlying permafrost. But as temperatures rise the permafrost recedes, (i)___the(ii)___of forest.
A. impairing
B. facilitating
C.decelerating
D. renewal
E. incursion
F. decline

分析: 选择BE. incursion 翻译成 进入,入侵. 前面说树少, 因为permafrost 冻土, 现在气温升高, 冻土 recede 减退, 就说明树 多了, 于是选 促进了树木的进入.

4.As late as the 1990s,Merry Wiesner Hanks argued that developments during the Reformation and the Enlightenment had (i)_____effects on the participation of women in the filed of medicine. She claimed that the (ii)___Reformation and Enlightenment women left female health’s practitioners restricted to assisting in an unpaid and unrecognized capacity.

A.dire adj.极其严重的,极糟的,极差的
B. positive
C.ambiguous

D. unchanging opportunities for
E. worsening positions of
F. reader respect for

  • the Reformation and the Enlightenment 文艺复兴和启蒙运动
    分析: 后面说的是 unpaid unrecognized 都是负面的词, 说明那个时期女性地位不高, 所以选择AE.

section 110

1.Characteristic of the diplomat’s new book is the____
relationship between the evidence adduced and the inferences drawn, the footnotes and citations teeming with ambiguity and complexity, while the summary statements are more dogmatic simplicities.

A. healthy
B. shifting
C. tenuous
D.compelling
E.plausible

首先 compelling 是 有说服力的, 而不是 矛盾的
分析句子: 后面的评价主要是 负面的, 又模糊, 又复杂, 又教条主义, 前面应该选一个比较负面的 tenuous 脆弱的 符合题意.
draw 这里取 draw a conclusion 的意思, 得出结论. 同样也可以得出 推断.

2.Notwithstanding a lack of____, nineteenth-century anthologies such as Evert and George Duyckinck’s Cyclopaedia of American Literature represents serious attempts to collect the national writings up to that point as a way of establishing the viability of American letters.

A. bias
B.earnestness
C. rigor n.严格
D.ambition
E.ambiguity n.摸棱两可

  • earnest adj.认真的,真挚的

Evert and George Duyckinck’s Cyclopaedia of American Literature 这整个是一本书.
notwithstanding 虽然, 这个挺重要的一个转折词.
后面说 这本书 代表了 严肃的尝试 取收集 国产的写作作品, 到了一个程度: 确立美国信件的可行性.

In his study of Senegambian cleric Shaykh Mass Kah (1827-1936), Bala S. K. Saho notes that it is difficult to fully (i)___ many of the oral accounts on which the study relies. Saho’s work shows.however, that despite this absence of (ii)___, oral history can provide useful sources from which historians can reconstruct the past.

A. comprehend
B. reproduce
C.validate

D. partiality
E. argumentation

For a genre that’s supposed to be about the future, science fiction has certainly (i)___lately,(ii)___is what sells best, with readers spending their money on sequels to long-running series.

A. become quite pessimistic
B. been looking backward
C. anticipated technological developments

section 107

1.Once so fluid, the political situation had, two years after the declaration of the Republic,____so much that further change seemed inconceivable.

A. revitalized
B. warmed
C. intensified
D. clotted
E. destabilized

  • conceive v.想象
  • inconceivable adj.不可思议的
  • clot v.凝结
  • destabilize v.使不稳定

句子分析: Once so fluid 修饰 政治局势, 后面有一个时间状语不用管, 再后面才是 谓语, 政治局势变得怎么样了, 前面是fluid流动, 后面说进一步的变化变得难以想象, 说明当前的政治局势变得 clot 凝结.

2.Paul Robeson.Jr. wrote that his father was a flesh-and-blood artist whose accomplishments made him susceptible to hagiographic treatment by potential biographers. Robeson achievements were real, and there was no need for____.

A. disclosure
B. hyperbole
C. retraction
D. muckraking
E. reticence

选 B. 选对了. 夸大.

  • hagiographic 理想化的, 偶像化的
  • flesh-and-blood 有血有肉的
  • disclosure n.揭露
  • reticent adj.缄默的
  • muckraking n.揭发丑闻
    muck,粪肥，raking,耙子，耙地。讽刺用法，最早来自美国总统罗斯福在1906 年的一次演讲 讽刺记者，后来被记者们用以自嘲并欣然接受该称呼。

The scientists who first proposed that Moon’s craters had resulted from impacts (i)__almost all of these craters were circular, and yet most impactors in heliocentric orbit would have an oblique path and hence would be expected to form (ii)___craters.

A. devised a theory
B. misinterpreted a finding
C. confronted difficulty
D. massive
E. elongated
F. shallow

  • crater n.火山口,坑
  • elongated adj.拉长了的,细长的
  • oblique adj.斜的
  • confront v.面对, 面临
  • devise v.设计,发明

选CE. 前面说所有坑都是圆形的, 后面说倾斜的轨道, 选项中又出现了 elongated 说明按照理论, 坑应该是长条形的, 前一个空自然选出 科学家们正在 面临一个难题.

When the United States government created the Post Office at the founding of the republic, it didn’t invite rival postal firms to compete; in fact, it created a monopoly. That monopoly, however, was (i)___free expression because of policies Congress adopted, which (ii)___the circulation of newspapers irrespective of their viewpoint and spread postal service throughout the country.

A. conducive to
B. threatened by
C. dependent on

D. subsidized
E. limited
F. trivialized

  • congress n.国会
  • conductive adj.有益的,有帮助的
  • circulation n.发行量
  • irrespective adj.不考虑,不顾
  • subsidize v.资助；补助；给…发津贴

单词题. 选AD. 前面说 垄断 反而 因为国会采纳的政策而 帮助了 自由言论. 后面说 国会具体是通过 资助报纸发行量(无论观点如何) 来帮助的.

With regard to verity, neutrality, and transparency, nothing about the Internet makes it any different than Gutenberg’s printing press, which could serve___just as well as truth.
A. fantasy
B. bureaucracy n.官僚制度
C. protocol
D.disinformation n.虚假信息
E.mendacity n.谎言
F. panic

解析：这个题只能根据just as well as truth来选，题目说也可以是事实，那么空格应该选事实的反义词，所以答案选DE选项。disinformation谎言，mendacity谎言。

翻译：关于真理，中立和透明，互联网的任何东西都和古腾堡的印刷机没啥大的区别，可能是谎言也可能是事实

  • detachment n.超然；超脱；冷漠

  1. The book captures the___of several politicians who spoke publicly of old-time virtues in order to mask private vices.
    A. probity
    B. dissemblance – dissemble v.掩盖，掩饰
    C. opportunism
    D. rectitude – n.正直；诚实 – rectangle n.长方形
    E. ingenuousness – ingenuous adj.单纯的；天真的
    F. duplicity n.奸诈
    • vice n.罪行,邪恶

    单词题, 选BF.

Upon realizing that the indicators of a stressful situation can be extremely___，the psychologist reconsidered her claim that a reliable way of reducing stress is to recognize stressful situations and then avoid them.

A. acute
B. exiguous
C. persistent
D. overstated
E. tenuous
F. unrelenting adj.持续的；不缓和的；势头不减的;不留情的

  • relent – v. to finally agree to sth after refusing 终于答应；不再拒绝 to become less determined, strong, etc.变缓和；变温和；减弱

解析：这个题是典型的结果推原因，关键词定位到reconsider，心理学家重新考虑她的言论，说明以前的言论有问题存在，再看看言论的内容“一个减压的可靠方式是识别出压力场景然后避免它们”，这个言论的问题就是这种压力场景无法识别，所以答案选BE选项。exiguous太少的，tenuous贫乏的。

翻译：在意识到压力场景的指示物可能是极其稀少之后，心理学家重新考虑了她的言论，言论的内容是“一个减压的可靠方式是识别出压力场景然后避免它们”。

For wily political press officers, the art of spin is not quite supplanting truth with lies, but instead it aspires to replace awkward complexity with catchy___:successful spin creates the impression of unavoidable common sense.

A. novelties
B. slogans
C. falsehoods n.虚假,说谎
D. duplicity
E. simplicity
F. intelligibility

  • spin v.n.（尤指有利于自己的）导向性陈述 – 算是熟词生意

虽然没看懂句子, 但是能看出来前后取反.

解析：句子最后提到成功的粉饰能够创造常识，既然是常识，所以特征就是大家都知道，所以答案选EF选项。simplicity简单化，intelligibility容易理解。

翻译：对于狡猾的政治出版社的官员来说，粉饰的艺术不会用谎言替代真相。相反它有志于用引人注意的可理解性来替代尴尬的复杂。成功的粉饰创造了不可避免的常识的印象。
